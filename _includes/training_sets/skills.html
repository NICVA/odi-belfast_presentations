Data Expeditions

The Storyteller

  • Responsible for ensuring that the story is interesting to a wide audience.
  • Asks complicated questions and ﬁnds interesting angles.
  • Maintains group focus on the key message.

The Scout

  • Responsible for hunting down data for use in the story.
  • Builds on or establishes key relationships with partners who are providing resources.
  • Should have a knowledge of data scraping and translation to diﬀerent formats.

The Analyst

  • Crunch data found by the scout.
  • Test storytellers hypothesis.
  • Discover new patterns in the data that could add more interesting angles to the story.

The Engineer

  • Responsible for the ﬁnal output being a functional service.
  • Doesn’t have to know how to code but in the majority of cases it is advised.
  • Should know how the web works.

The Designer

  • Makes sure the story comes to life.
  • Works with the story teller to ensure an emotional connection with intended audience.
  • (A designer who just produces a paper version of the output is just as valid!)
