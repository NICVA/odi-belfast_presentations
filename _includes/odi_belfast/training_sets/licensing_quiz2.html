Can you download and open it?

Exercise

Can you publish an extract from the database?

Yes, so long as it is not "substantial"

Exercise

Can you add a new column and republish the dataset on your website?

No, even if you attribute you have no rights to republish

Exercise

Can you add analyse it and derive new data?

Maybe. As long as it is not possible to reconstruct the database, and there are no restrictions (e.g. no derivatives licence)

Can you create a subset of the dataset and publish on your own website?

Yes, as long as you attribute and attach a simliar licence

Exercise

Can you add new elements to the dataset and republish?

Yes (so long as you're not adding other copyright content)

Can you mix the dataset with you own data and sell it on?

Yes, there are no commercial restictions, but you have to share-alike
